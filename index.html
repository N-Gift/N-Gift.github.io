Building a Better World Together

N-word passes are designed to turn a bad thing into a good thing. Our passes are perfect for family gatherings, corporate events, and parties, allowing people to get creative and come together in a unique and meaningful way. Our mission is to make the world a better place, one pass at a time.

The N-Word Pass: Hitting Racism Where It Hurts

Through these passes, people are charged a fee every time they use the n-word, with the proceeds going to support social justice causes. The money raised from the passes goes towards charities and causes which combat racism and empower people of color.

Spreading Kindness and Hope to Our Community

N-gift uses n-word passes to give back to charity. Our innovative platform allows us to donate to a variety of organizations and causes that are meaningful to us. Our mission is to spread kindness and hope to those who are in need and to make a positive difference in the lives of those around us.
